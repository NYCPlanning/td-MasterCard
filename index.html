MasterCard Transaction Count Index Trends

Total Retail Transaction Count Index

April 2020:
Total credit card transactions in April 2020 were substantially below total transactions in April 2019.​ The city’s business, tourism, and recreationally-oriented neighborhoods saw the greatest declines, with total transactions down by more than 75% in many of the city’s zip codes.​

April 2021:
One year after the start of the pandemic in NYC, many of the city’s residentially oriented neighborhoods saw modest to substantial increases in total transactions relative to pre-pandemic.​ Increases likely coincide with more people continuing to work – and spend – close to home.​

April 2022:
More recently (data from early April 2022), the city’s CBDs continue to see total credit card transactions more closely resembling those from before the pandemic.​ Many neighborhoods outside the CBDs continue to see more credit card transactions than they saw pre-pandemic.​

April 2021:
